Free Checkup

We offer regular free health screening camps to help you detect early signs of disease. Screenings include basic vitals, diabetes and blood pressure checks.

  • Basic physical exam
  • Blood pressure & glucose check
  • Doctor consultation and advice
Cardiogram & Cardiac Care

Our cardiology unit provides ECG, stress testing and full cardiac evaluation backed by experienced cardiologists and on-site monitoring.

  • ECG and stress testing
  • Cardiac risk assessment
  • Follow-up care plans
DNA Testing Services

Accurate and confidential DNA testing for diagnostics and family-related tests, performed in certified labs.

  • Paternity & kinship testing
  • Genetic screening where required
  • Secure sample handling
Blood Bank Services

Our blood bank ensures safe and timely availability of blood and components for emergency and planned procedures.

  • 24/7 blood availability
  • Component separation and storage
  • Safe transfusion protocols
